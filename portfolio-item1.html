Price Panel

Goals:

Displaying price packages in a simple and clear manner for Spotify. Attracting new users and payed subscribers. (Spotify, if you happen to be here and like it, please accept it.)

Colors & Font:

I created a classic green-based color scheme to create a high-end, professional look that is consistent with the logo of Spotify.

I chose this bright green so that I could use it to draw attention to important text and calls to action.

I used Open Sans for all text. It keeps the panel in consistency.
